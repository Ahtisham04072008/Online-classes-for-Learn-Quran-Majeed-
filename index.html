Learn Quran with Tajweed

Tajweed means the Arabic word for beautification. Reciting the Quran under Tajweed sets the correct way to pronounce and recite its verses. It means each letter in the Quran is read with its proper rights and qualities when someone recites. So, each Muslim should make sure to learn the Holy Quran online following the proper Tajweed rules.
