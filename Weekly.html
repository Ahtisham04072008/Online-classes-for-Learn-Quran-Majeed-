Weekly Progress Evaluation

At Madarsa Tul Quran, we believe that continuous assessment is the key to Quranic excellence. Our Weekly Progress Evaluation system helps students and parents stay updated on learning outcomes, strengths, and areas for improvement.

  • ✅ Weekly teacher feedback reports
  • ✅ Tajweed accuracy checks
  • ✅ Fluency and pronunciation review
  • ✅ Class participation & homework performance
  • ✅ Personalized suggestions for growth

Our dedicated quality assurance team works closely with tutors to ensure every student is progressing at their best pace. These evaluations help motivate learners and create a path toward becoming excellent reciters of the Quran.
